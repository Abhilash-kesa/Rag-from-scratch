Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase. Refunds are processed
to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Digital products are non-refundable once downloaded, except in cases of technical
defects verified by our support team.

Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 5-10 business days within the continental US. Express
shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee, delivering
within 2-3 business days.
